Simple SEO Search Engine Demo

Demonstrating crawling, indexing, ranking, and SEO principles

Try searching for: "plant" (finds plants, planting, planted), "cook" (finds cooking, cooked), "travel" (finds traveling, traveled), "photo" (finds photography), "garden" (finds gardening, gardens)

This demo showcases how search engines crawl, index, and rank web pages based on SEO factors like title tags, meta descriptions, content frequency, and backlink authority.
