Authentic Chicken Biryani Recipe by Deion Bhai

Deion Bhai's famous chicken biryani recipe uses traditional dum cooking methods passed down through generations. This authentic biryani recipe ensures every grain of basmati rice is perfectly cooked and infused with aromatic spices.

Essential Biryani Ingredients

  • Basmati rice - Premium quality for the best biryani texture
  • Fresh chicken - Marinated in yogurt and biryani spices
  • Saffron - For authentic biryani color and aroma
  • Biryani masala - Deion Bhai's signature spice blend

Deion Bhai's Biryani Cooking Techniques

The secret to Deion Bhai's world-class biryani lies in the traditional dum cooking method. This slow-cooking technique allows the biryani flavors to develop naturally, creating the perfect harmony between rice, meat, and spices.

Step-by-Step Biryani Preparation

  1. Marinate chicken in biryani spices and yogurt for 2 hours
  2. Soak premium basmati rice in water for 30 minutes
  3. Layer marinated chicken and partially cooked rice in heavy-bottomed pot
  4. Seal with aluminum foil and cook using dum method for 45 minutes
  5. Let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before serving
