Restaurant Review

Went to this restaurant yesterday. They serve biryani and other dishes.

The food was okay. Service was slow but the waiter was nice. Place was crowded.

Biryani had good flavor but rice was little dry. Chicken was tender though.

Price is reasonable for the portion size. Around $12 for chicken biryani.

Would probably go back again sometime. Location is convenient for me.

Overall rating: 3 out of 5 stars. Not bad but not amazing either.
